About APJ Abdual Kalam Life

  1. Educational and scientific institutions Several educational and scientific institutions and other locations were renamed or named in honour of Kalam following his death.
  2. An agricultural college at Kishanganj, Bihar, was renamed the "Dr. Kalam Agricultural College, Kishanganj" by the Bihar state government on the day of Kalam's funeral. The state government also announced it would name a proposed science city after Kalam.
  3. Uttar Pradesh Technical University (UPTU) was renamed "A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University" by the Uttar Pradesh state government.
  4. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Memorial Travancore Institute of Digestive Diseases, a new research institute in Kollam city, Kerala attached to the Travancore Medical College Hospital.
  5. A new academic complex at Mahatma Gandhi University in Kerala.
  6. A new science centre and planetarium in Lawspet, Puducherry.
  7. India and the US have launched the Fulbright-Kalam Climate Fellowship In September 2014.   8. Kerala Technological University, headquartered at Thiruvananthapuram where Kalam lived for years, was renamed to A P J Abdul Kalam Technological University after his death.
